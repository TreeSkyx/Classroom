Teyvat's Zoo Directors

Paimon found that in the previous year, a total of 334,101 animals were caught and re-created with the "Omni-Ubiquity Net" every day. And it seems that these creatures have been taken home to the Travelers' Serenitea Pot. Our data shows that there are 505 Serenitea Pots that are home to more than 200 animals!
Dogs and cats seem to be the most popular animals among Travelers. So the question must be asked: are there more dogs or cats in the Serenitea Pots? According to Paimon's survey, Serenitea Pots have 13% more cats' than dogs, a victory for cat lovers!
